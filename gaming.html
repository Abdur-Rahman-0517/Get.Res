How to Play

  • Click START GAME to begin (camera access required)
  • Point your TV remote at your webcam and press any button
  • The game will detect the IR light from your remote as a "shot"
  • Aim at the red target and shoot to score points
  • Each hit gives you 10 points
  • The target will move to a new position after each hit
  • Complete all targets before time runs out!
  • Note: Your webcam needs to be able to detect IR light
IR Detection Tip: Most webcams have IR filters, but some older models and security cameras can detect IR light.
